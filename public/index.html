Help & Documentation

Getting Started

Follow these steps to start boosting your Facebook shares:

  1. Obtain your Facebook AppState
  2. Copy the post URL you want to boost
  3. Set your desired share amount and interval
  4. Click "Start Boost" to begin

Safety Guidelines

  • Always use secondary accounts
  • Keep intervals reasonable (5-10 seconds recommended)
  • Don't exceed 1000 shares per session

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues:

  • Verify your AppState is valid
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the post URL is public
